Iterative Design for Banana App
Context
Bananas is a grocery delivery startup based in Jakarta, Indonesia where you can order groceries and get it delivered in minutes. Its aim is to deliver groceries directly to user's home within minutes and at supermarket prices.
Iterative design
After we made an interactive high-fidelity (hi-fi) prototype based on the wireframe we created, we received a round of critiques. A interesting point is that many button looks clickable but infact they are not.
User Testing
we conducted our own usability test through a remote user testing service (UserTesting.com), using our final interactive Hi-Fi prototype. We provided testing instructions, some specific tasks as well as post-test questions to collect information from three users.
Conclusion
We received many valueble feedbacks that we've never thought of and I think I should stick with collecting feedbacks for the future projects I will build.
